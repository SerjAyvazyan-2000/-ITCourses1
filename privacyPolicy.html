Privacy Policy

At AU English IT Courses, we are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with transparency, integrity, and responsibility.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information from you when you:

  • Register on our website
  • Subscribe to our newsletters or content
  • Enroll in courses or programs
  • Contact us via email, phone, or forms

This may include your name, email address, phone number, location, and course preferences.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our courses and services
  • Improve user experience and learning outcomes
  • Send updates, promotions, and educational content
  • Respond to inquiries and offer support
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your data. We may share it only with:

  • Educational partners for course delivery
  • Payment processors (e.g., Stripe, PayPal)
  • Analytics platforms (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Government or legal entities when required by law

4. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

5. Security Measures

We use technical, administrative, and physical security measures to protect your data, including SSL encryption, firewalls, and restricted access to personal information.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communication
  • Submit a complaint to a data protection authority

7. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to understand site usage and improve performance. Learn more in our Cookie Policy.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy as necessary to reflect legal, technical, or business changes. The most recent version will always be posted here with the effective date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us:
